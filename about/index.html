About

Hello! I'm Vikrant, a product designer based in Slough, UK.

My journey as a designer hasn’t been linear, but looking back, the dots connect. I’ve had a fascination with visual design since I was a kid. I vividly remember playing around in MS Paint and even pretending to design websites in MS Word! As I got older, I taught myself how to use design software and spent years dabbling in graphic design. Fun fact: I once spotted one of my old logo designs while driving on a motorway!

When it came to choosing a career, I found myself in marketing – as I was looking for a field that combined creativity with business. That’s where I honed my understanding of people, messaging, and the broader customer experience. Even as a marketer, I often stepped into help with design-related tasks, thanks to the skills and design-thinking I’d developed up over the years. Because of that, I got to work closely with UX designers on projects including user research, journey mapping, developer collaboration, wire framing, and creating digital content. 

Eventually, I realised I wanted to do more than support design – I wanted to DO design. So, I decided to transition my career and retrained through the UX Design Institute. Now, I’m looking forward to making an impact in my first, official design role!

If you'd like to learn more, offer feedback, or discuss a project/opportunity, please get in touch via e-mail, LinkedIn, or X.
